About AES

Abantu Environmental Services

About Us

Abantu Environmental Services (Pty) Ltd is a multidisciplinary consultancy specializing in environmental management, waste planning, GIS, remote sensing, and scientific research. Established in 2018, the 100% youth-owned Level 1 BBBEE company is dedicated to sustainable development and job creation in rural communities across the Eastern Cape.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading consultancy and research firm that delivers integrated solutions to complex socio-economic and environmental challenges while fostering sustainable employment and skills development in the Eastern Cape.
